http://www.poesi.as/index.htm

http://literature.wnso.org/2002/munamadan.htm

PS:他的另两首诗(尼原文)
http://literature.wnso.org/2002/yatri.htm
http://literature.wnso.org/2002/sansar_rupi.htm
