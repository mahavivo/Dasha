没有别的意思，Dasha最近看MHD的文字，已经分不出拼写的对错啦

没有别的意思，Dasha最近看MHD的文字，已经分不出拼写的对错啦“Wo die Weingaerten beginnen der schmale / Weg absteigt sich……”句，傻以为，“在那葡萄園之邊”，不妥，此句似乎表示“路从葡萄园开始（向外延伸）”；“der schmale / Weg”译“曲径”，亦似乎属于“过度诠释”，“细路”、“窄路”……但未必是“弯曲的路”。此外，“曲径通幽”，美则美矣，却失去了原作者的匠意：absteigt＝absteigen，“下行”之义，die absteigende Tonleiter，就是音乐术语里的“下行音阶”＝“7654321”，所以作者后文有拾柴女“buecken”（弯腰）的比喻。

期待Pilger兄的好文字。

贴几个Rita Dove和Fred Viebahn的英译

贴几个Rita Dove和Fred Viebahn的英译Winter music

A red fox I was
Once leaping high
Took what I wanted.
Gray that's me now gray rain.
I made it to Greenland
In my heart.
On the coast a stone shines
Its inscription: Nobody comes back.
The stone shortens my life.
The four corners of the world
Are full of sorrow. Love
Is like the snapping of the spine.

Free verses

Last night I awoke knew
That I should say goodbye now
To these verses. That's how it always goes
After a few years. They have to get out
Into the world. It's not possible to keep them
Forever! here under the roof.
Poor things. They must set out for town.
A few will be allowed to return later.
But most of them are still hanging around out there.
Who knows what will become of them. Before they
Find their peace.

Wiepersdorf

This evening, Bettina, it's like
The old days. We're always
Alone when we write to kings,
Those of the heart and those
Of the state. And yet
Our heart skips a beat
When on the other side of the house
A car can be heard.

Translated by Rita Dove & Fred Viebahn

SARAH KIRSCH(*) was born in 1945 in Limlingrode, and studied
biology and literature in Leipzig. She moved to West Berlin in 1977
and since 1983 has lived in Schleswig-Holstein. Her most recent
poetry publications are Erlkonigs Tochter (1992) and Bodenlos and
Ich, Crusoe (1996). She has been awarded the Holderlin, Huchel, and
Buchner Prizes.

发表于Poetry1998年10-11月号。
